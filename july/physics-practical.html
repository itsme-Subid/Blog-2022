Physics Practical

Date: July 28 2022
This is a physics practical experiments. This experiments is based on the physics practical of our syllabus. This was dictated by the Physics teacher (Atanu Chaudhuri of SNV) and written by Subid Das.

Section - A

  •   1. To find resistance of a given wire using meterbridge.

  •   2. To determine resistance per cm of given wire by plotting a graph of potential difference versus current.

  •   3. To verify the laws of combination (series or parallel) of resistances using a meter bridge.

  •   4. To compare emf of two given primary cells using potentiometer.

  •   5. To determine the internal resistance of given primary cell using potentiometer.

  •   6. To determine the resistance of a galvanometer by half deflection method and to find its figure of merit.

  •   7. To convert the galvanometer of known resistance and figure of merit.

  •   8. To find the frequency of the AC mains using sonometer.

Section - B

  •   1. To find the value of V for different values of u in case of concave mirror and to find out the focal length.

  •   2. To find the focal length of a convex mirror using a convex lens.

  •   3. To find the focal length of convex lens by plotting graph between u and v and 1⁄u and 1⁄v .

  •   4. To find the focal lenth of concave lens using convex lens.

  •   5. To determine angle of incidence and angle of deviation.

  •   6. To determine refractive index of glass slab using a travelling microscope.

  •   7. To determine refractive index of a liquid by using:

    • Concave mirror,
    • Concave lens, and
    • Plane mirror.

  •   8. To draw the I-V characteristic curve of a p-n junction in forward bias and reverse bias.

  •   9. To draw the characteristic curve of a zener diode and to determine its reverse break down voltage.

  •   10. To study the charateristic of a common emitter NPN or PNP and to find out the value and voltage gains.

Note: Checked experiments are most like to be done, if more time left then other experiments will be done.
